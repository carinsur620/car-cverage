PrimeShield Terms & Conditions

By purchasing a PrimeShield vehicle protection plan, you agree to the following terms and conditions.

---

1. Coverage Start

Coverage begins 7 days after purchase. No claims may be submitted during this initial waiting period.

This policy helps ensure fairness and prevents misuse of the service.

---

2. Coverage Limits

  • Basic Plan: Covers up to $1,000 per claim
  • Standard Plan: Covers up to $2,500 per claim
  • Premium Plan: Covers up to $4,000 per claim
---

3. Deductible

Customers are responsible for a deductible starting at $250 per claim before coverage applies.

---

4. Exclusions

The following are NOT covered:

  • Pre-existing vehicle issues
  • Damage due to neglect or misuse
  • Routine maintenance (oil changes, brakes, etc.)
  • Accidents or external damage
---

5. Claims Process

All claims must be submitted through our official dashboard. Approval is required before any repair work is completed.

---

6. Payment & Refunds

All payments are processed securely via PayPal. Refund requests may be reviewed and are subject to approval.

---

7. Cancellation

You may cancel your plan at any time. Coverage will end at the end of the billing cycle.

---

8. Agreement

By purchasing a plan, you acknowledge and agree to all terms listed above.
